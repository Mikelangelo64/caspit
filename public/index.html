Group of companies

The company’s major business lines are petrochemistry, liquefied gas, renewable energy and fertilizers. Caspit has its own production and logistics infrastructure.

From a group of traders to a high-capacity holding

Nowadays we are sequentially developing Caspit and its product lines in CIS region. Our robust infrastructure is of an utmost importance in such an endeavor. We deliver the goods via sheer use of our own capabilities, with no intermediaries involved. Thus, we are able to commit to ironclad guarantees from our side, securing the production quality and prompt transportation of the goods.

Petrochemicals

SBPM operates in domain of petrochemicals and fertilizers wholesale, while simultaneously strengthening partnership ties with agribusiness. Recently we've set up a licensed customs warehouse of excise goods plus two warehouses with bulk chemistry in Boryspil (Kyiv) and Rivno. SPBM handles a vehicle fleet of 40 tank-vehicles.

Financing

LLC Eximleasing is a financial company that meets the needs of farmers in financing equipment, fertilizers, seed materials, and more.

Solar energy

ONG Solar designs, constructs and operates solar power plants for industrial sites.

Fertilizers

Own production of Dunger mineral and organomineral fertilizers in Ukraine

Agronovation provides a service for introducing technical ammonia into the soil, the project is being implemented in Kazakhstan

Logistics

Cargo Supply enterprise enables our portfolio companies to ship the LPG units to whatever furthest needed destinations. Company disposes gas carriers and tanks to move bulk chemistry units.

Advanced infrastructure
